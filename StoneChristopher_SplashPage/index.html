Friends is a show based in Manhattan,NY about 6 friends and their comedic and romantic adventures through life. Everything from job life, to marriage and kids, they really get to experience it all together. They grow a family like friendship that goes on a wild roller coaster ride as Rachel, Monica, Phoebe, Joey, Chandler, and Ross experience all walks of life.
